PROJECT: SNAPCHAT AUTOMATION + VIRAL CONTENT SYSTEM

GOAL:
Build a fully automated system that:

* Finds viral videos from platforms (YouTube, Douyin, TikTok...)
* Downloads and processes them
* Adds custom music
* Uploads to Snapchat using automation
* Tracks performance and scales winning content

---

1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

---

Pipeline:

[Fetch Viral Videos]
↓
[Download Video]
↓
[Edit + Add Music]
↓
[Upload Snapchat]
↓
[Track Performance]
↓
[Scale Winning Content]

---

2. CORE MODULES

---

2.1 VIRAL VIDEO SCRAPER

Purpose:

* Collect viral video links automatically

Sources:

* YouTube Shorts
* Douyin / TikTok
* Instagram Reels (optional)

Functions:

* Search by keyword (e.g. "sad edit", "viral clip", "anime edit")
* Get video URLs
* Filter by:

  * views > threshold
  * duration < 60s

Tech:

* YouTube: yt-dlp or unofficial scraping
* TikTok/Douyin: API scraping or headless browser

Output:

* List of video URLs stored in DB

---

2.2 VIDEO DOWNLOADER

Purpose:

* Download videos from collected URLs

Tech:

* yt-dlp (recommended)

Functions:

* Download best quality
* Save metadata (title, views)

---

2.3 VIDEO PROCESSING MODULE

Purpose:

* Transform content to avoid duplication detection

Processing:

* Cut clip (5–15s)
* Resize (9:16)
* Add subtitle/text overlay
* Add zoom or blur effect
* Add background music (your music)

Tech:

* ffmpeg (core)
* moviepy (optional)

---

2.4 MUSIC MANAGEMENT

Purpose:

* Manage your music library

Functions:

* Store music files
* Match music with video theme
* Upload to distributor (Ditto)

---

2.5 SNAPCHAT AUTOMATION

Purpose:

* Upload processed videos

Tech:

* Playwright (async)

Functions:

* Login account
* Upload video
* Add music
* Publish

Rules:

* 3–5 videos/account/day
* Random delay (30s–120s)

---

2.6 PROXY ROTATION

Purpose:

* Avoid detection

Rules:

* 1 account = 1 proxy
* Rotate proxy if failed

---

2.7 JOB QUEUE SYSTEM

Purpose:

* Manage workflow

Jobs:

* scrape_video
* download_video
* process_video
* upload_video

Features:

* Retry (max 3 times)
* Status tracking (pending, running, failed, done)

---
